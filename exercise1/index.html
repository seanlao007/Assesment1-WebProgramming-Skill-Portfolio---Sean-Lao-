Classic Sean Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup sugar & 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs & 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 1/4 cups flour, 1 tsp baking soda, 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix ingredients, then fold in chocolate chips.
  3. Drop dough onto sheets.
  4. Bake for 9-11 minutes.

Fudgy Brownies

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a small baking pan.
  2. Whisk the melted butter and sugar together, then beat in the eggs.
  3. Gently stir in the cocoa powder and flour until just combined.
  4. Pour into the pan and bake for 20-25 minutes.
