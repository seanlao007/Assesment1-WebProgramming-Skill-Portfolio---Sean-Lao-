The Plot

Tian Yu, a struggling writer from Hong Kong, travels to Taiwan in search of a legendary location known as the 'Bay of Vanishing Whales.' Along the way, he meets a local guide named A-Xiang (Teng, 2024).

The two embark on a road trip that leads them to discover the beauty of the island and a mysterious connection that transcends time and space.
